Operational Framework

Clear contributions. Transparent process. Member-first support.

Membership & Contributions

1

Registration

New members join by filling out the interest form and paying a one-time yearly registration fee of KES 500. This confirms your commitment to the welfare constitution.

2

Monthly Contributions

Every member contributes KES 200 monthly. This amount is payable by the 5th of every month. Consistent contribution is required to remain in good standing and eligible for benefits.

3

Support Requests & Review

When a need arises, members submit a formal request to the committee. The request is reviewed against the constitution, a decision is communicated promptly, and the outcome is recorded for transparency.

Governance & Accountability

Our welfare is managed by a democratically elected committee to ensure funds are safe and used for their intended purpose.

Leadership
Chairperson

Oversees all welfare activities, presides over meetings, and ensures the constitution is upheld.

Administration
Secretary

Maintains records of meetings, membership registry, and communicates updates to the team.

Finance
Treasurer

Collects contributions, maintains financial records, and prepares financial reports for transparency.

Community
Committee Members

Represent the interests of the general members and assist in decision-making processes.
