About Our Community

More than just colleagues—we are a family.

Our Mission

To foster a community of unity and mutual support where members stand by each other during times of need. We are dedicated to upholding transparency in our operations and promoting a culture of shared member responsibility for the well-being of the entire team.

Quick Facts

  • Registration: KES 500 yearly
  • Monthly contribution: KES 200 (due by 5th)
  • Member portal: announcements + contributions + updates

Our Objectives

  • To provide financial and moral support during emergencies and bereavements.
  • To organize social events that strengthen team bonding.
  • To promote a culture of saving and financial discipline among members.
  • To ensure transparency and accountability in managing member contributions.

How Support is Handled

We operate on a structured support system designed for fairness and speed. When a member faces a qualifying event, they submit a request to the committee. This is reviewed against our constitution to ensure eligibility, and upon approval, support is disbursed promptly. Every transaction is documented to maintain full transparency and trust within the group.

Core Values

Empathy

We understand and share the feelings of our colleagues.

Integrity

We manage funds with absolute honesty and transparency.

Unity

We believe that we are stronger when we stand together.
